Legend of the Tongue Tied Society

Centuries ago, there lived the most prosperous society the world has ever seen. The society consisted of four Сourts: the Hearts, the Diamonds, the Spades, and the Clubs - each representing a different strength: beauty, wisdom, courage, and intellect. Within each of these Сourts lived the writers, artists, musicians, healers, engineers, and leaders of the world.

There lived a man on the outside, the Joker, whose jealousy grew as none of the Сourts accepted him. One night, as the courts danced together in one of their frequent magical parties, the Joker poisoned the flowing champagne with a special tongue curse. From that day forward, each time the members tried to speak about their beloved Сourts and society, they couldn't find the words. They were tongue tied.

Over the centuries, the memory faded as the stories stopped being told, yet quietly this society remained among us. Every now and then, when words escape you and you feel tongue tied, it's the curse weakening. At night, when your dreams take you to a magical place that seems too good to be true, thats our world, calling you home.

One day, a Queen was born with powers so strong that she could remember. She knew she had to reverse the Joker's curse and reignite this glorious society, so she created the deck of cards, with each Сourt's symbol hand painted as a calling to anyone else in the world who may remember. After years of relentless work, her cards fell into the hands of a long lost King, who had been hopeless, believing the society was gone forever. He found his Queen of Hearts and together they created a magical deck of cards that reversed the curse. These cards await you, so draw one for reintroduction into your Сourt. Unlock your membership in the most prosperous society the world has ever seen.

About the Artist

Alexandra Houx Grounds is a young, female, American oil painter specializing in large scale portraiture. Her hyper-realistic technique merges with her surrealist style to create one of a kind paintings that often depict classic iconic females and peers, also closer examinations of the human body. Grounds is a pioneer in female self-representation, and has forged her own path in the art world.

Her bold, vibrant work and direct-to-collector approach have landed her paintings in the most prestigious private art collections in the world. She has long believed that blockchain technology is the future of art, revolutionizing how people interact, collect, sell, and learn about artists and their work. Grounds currently has work displayed at the World Trade Center as well as the museum at Castello di Ugento in Puglia, Italy.

What is the Tongue Tied Society?

The Tongue Tied Society is an NFT collection and social club on Solana. There are 5200 unique playing cards, or 100 decks of cards, in the collection. A Tongue Tied Society NFT is your ticket into our idyllic ancient society and grants you access to exclusive upcoming events and future drops.

How Do I Mint?

1. Create a Phantom Wallet
If you are creating a Phantom wallet for the first time, make sure you write down or store your 12-word seed phrase somewhere safe in case you need to restore your wallet in the future!

2. Purchase some SOL
We recommend purchasing SOL on Coinbase, FTX, or Binance depending on your location!

3. Transfer SOL to your Phantom Wallet
You can copy your Phantom wallet address simply by clicking on the name of your wallet at the top of the Phantom plug-in window. Send SOL to this address.

4. Connect your Phantom wallet by clicking "Connect" on TongueTiedSociety.com
Before connecting, ensure the web address is correct. This is our only official website. Stay vigilant to avoid scams!

5. Click MINT and approve the transaction in your Phantom pop-up
Now you are able to mint your unique Tongue Tied Society NFT!
